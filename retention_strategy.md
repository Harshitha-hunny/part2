**Cluster 3 (loyal customers):**

**Cluster 4 (Potential Big Spenders):**

Characteristics: moderate-high Recency, moderate Frequency, high Monetary, moderate Discount Usage, high Category Diversity. These customers spend a good amount and explore categories but don't purchase as
